Tickets

Tickets are sold according to availability and give access to the conference, meals and snacks between 8:30am and 6pm.
Transportation and accommodation are not included.
Need for a particular fare due to a particular situation (studies, unemployment, media relations…)? Please, contact us.

Payment

You can register and pay for your tickets by credit card, online.
Within three weeks of the event, you can also book your tickets online and pay within two weeks by bank transfer.
If you need it, the Weezevent platform will provide you with an invoice.
All purchases are firm and final.
